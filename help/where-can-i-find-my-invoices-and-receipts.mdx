# Where can I find my invoices and receipts?

Invoices and receipts are typically available in your billing or account settings. If you do not see them, first confirm that your role includes billing access.

<Callout kind="info">

Invoices and receipts are usually available from your billing or account settings. Workspace owners or billing admins typically have access to download them.

</Callout>

## Check billing or account settings

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open your billing or account settings">
    Go to your account area and look for **Billing**, **Account settings**, or a similar section where payment details are managed.

    If invoices or receipts are available, they are typically listed there.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Look for invoices or receipts">
    Check for a section labeled **Invoices**, **Billing history**, **Receipts**, or similar.

    If your workspace provides access, you should see past billing documents with an option to open or download them.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Confirm your role if nothing appears">
    If you cannot find invoices or receipts, confirm whether you are a **workspace owner** or **billing admin**.

    In many workspaces, only those roles can view or download billing documents.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Who can download invoices and receipts

Workspace owners or billing admins usually have access to download invoices and receipts. If you do not have one of those roles, ask a workspace owner or billing admin to retrieve the documents for you.
