# How do I connect a third-party tool?

Most third-party tools can be connected from your workspace settings or while you are building a workflow. Choose the app, sign in to your account, and approve the requested permissions to finish the connection.

## Where to start

Start from **workspace settings** if you want to manage connections before building. If you are already creating a workflow, you can usually connect the tool directly during setup.

<Callout kind="info">

You may need the right workspace access or admin approval to connect some tools, depending on how your organization manages external accounts.

</Callout>

## Connect the tool

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open integrations">

  Go to your workspace settings or the workflow builder, then open the integrations area.

  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose the app">

  Select the third-party tool you want to connect.

  </Step>
  <Step title="Authenticate your account">

  Sign in to the tool when prompted and continue through the authorization flow.

  </Step>
  <Step title="Approve permissions">

  Review the requested permissions and confirm access to complete the connection.

  </Step>
</Steps>

## Confirm the integration is active

After the connection finishes, the tool should appear as connected in your workspace or workflow. If the integration is active, you can select it during workflow setup without being asked to reconnect.

## If authorization fails

If the connection does not complete, start the authorization flow again and make sure you sign in with the correct account. If your organization restricts app connections, confirm that you have permission to authorize the tool or ask an admin to review the access settings.
