## FAQs

<ExpandableGroup>
  <Expandable title="Does AetherFlow support SSO or SAML?">
    Yes. Enterprise plans typically include support for single sign-on using common identity standards such as SAML. This helps larger organizations centralize authentication and access management.
  </Expandable>

  <Expandable title="Can we set advanced permissions for different teams?">
    Yes. Enterprise workspaces usually support role-based access controls so admins can manage who can view, edit, approve, or manage workflows and settings.
  </Expandable>

  <Expandable title="Are audit logs available for enterprise customers?">
    Audit logging is commonly available for enterprise environments that need stronger visibility into account activity, workspace changes, and administrative actions.
  </Expandable>

  <Expandable title="Can your team help with security reviews or questionnaires?">
    Yes. Enterprise prospects and customers often work with the AetherFlow team during security reviews, vendor assessments, and procurement processes.
  </Expandable>

  <Expandable title="Is onboarding support included for enterprise accounts?">
    In many cases, yes. Enterprise onboarding may include implementation guidance, rollout planning, and coordination with internal stakeholders to help teams launch smoothly.
  </Expandable>

  <Expandable title="How long do custom contracts or procurement usually take?">
    Timelines vary by organization, but enterprise purchasing often takes longer than self-serve signup because it can involve legal review, security review, and procurement approval steps.
  </Expandable>
</ExpandableGroup>
