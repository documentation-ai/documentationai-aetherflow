# When does billing start?

Billing usually starts when your free trial ends or when you move onto a paid plan. If you need the exact date for your account, check your billing settings or the checkout summary you saw when upgrading.

<Callout kind="info">

</Callout>

## Where to confirm your billing start date

Check your **billing settings** for the most accurate start date tied to your account. If you recently upgraded, review your **checkout summary** as well, since it should show when paid billing begins.

The exact start date can vary based on when your trial ends or when you switch plans, so these account-specific details are the best source of truth.

## What to do next

If you are unsure when charges will begin, open your billing settings first. If you upgraded during checkout, compare that information with your checkout details to confirm the date shown there.
