# What is AetherFlow?

AetherFlow is an AI-powered workflow automation platform that allows you to create automations using natural language. Instead of writing complex code or using drag-and-drop interfaces, you simply describe what you want to automate in plain English, and our AI engine handles the rest.

## How It Works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Describe Your Workflow">
    Tell AetherFlow what you want to automate in plain English
  </Step>
  <Step title="AI Generates Steps">
    Our AI analyzes your description and creates the workflow steps
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review & Test">
    Review the generated workflow and test it with sample data
  </Step>
  <Step title="Go Live">
    Activate your workflow to start automating tasks
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Key Features

- **Natural Language Processing**: Describe workflows in plain English
- **50+ Integrations**: Connect with popular business applications
- **AI-Powered**: No coding or complex setup required
- **Real-Time Execution**: Workflows run automatically when triggered
- **Visual Monitoring**: Track performance and execution logs

## Example Workflows

**"When I receive an email from a customer, summarize it and post the summary to our Slack channel"**

This creates a workflow that:
1. Monitors your email inbox
2. Uses AI to summarize incoming customer emails
3. Automatically posts the summary to your team's Slack channel

**"Every Monday at 9 AM, generate a weekly sales report and email it to the management team"**

This creates a workflow that:
1. Runs on a schedule (every Monday at 9 AM)
2. Pulls data from your CRM system
3. Generates a formatted report
4. Emails it to specified recipients
