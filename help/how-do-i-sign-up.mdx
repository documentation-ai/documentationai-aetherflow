# How do I sign up for AetherFlow?

Getting started with AetherFlow is quick and easy. Follow these steps to create your account and begin automating workflows.

## Quick Signup Process

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit our website">
    Go to [aetherflow.com](https://aetherflow.com) and click the "Sign Up" button
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose your method">
    Create an account using your email address or sign up with Google, Microsoft, or other OAuth providers
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify your email">
    Check your email for a verification link and click to activate your account
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete your profile">
    Add your name, company details, and preferences
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info">
  **No credit card required for signup!** Start your 14-day free trial without entering payment information. All Pro features are available during the trial period.
</Callout>

## Account Options

### Email Signup
- Create a new account with your email address
- Set a secure password
- Email verification required

### OAuth Providers
- **Google**: One-click signup with your Google account
- **Microsoft**: Use your Microsoft 365 or Azure AD account
- **SSO**: Enterprise customers can use company SSO

## Free Trial Benefits

Your 14-day free trial includes:

- ✅ Unlimited workflows
- ✅ 50,000 executions per month
- ✅ All 25+ Pro integrations
- ✅ Email support
- ✅ Advanced features
- ✅ No credit card required

## After Signup

Once your account is created:

1. **Welcome email** with getting started resources
2. **Dashboard access** to create your first workflow
3. **Integration setup** - connect your favorite tools
4. **Documentation access** for detailed guides
