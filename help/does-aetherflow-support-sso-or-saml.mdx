# Does AetherFlow support SSO or SAML?

AetherFlow supports single sign-on for organizations that need centralized control over how users authenticate and access the platform.

<Callout kind="info">

Enterprise plans typically include support for single sign-on using common identity standards such as SAML.

</Callout>

## Why this matters for larger organizations

SSO helps larger teams manage authentication in one place instead of maintaining separate login processes across tools. That makes it easier to align AetherFlow access with your organization's existing identity and security practices.

## Benefits of centralized authentication and access management

Centralized authentication gives admins a more consistent way to manage who can sign in and how access is controlled. It also reduces the overhead of managing credentials separately for each user account.

With SSO and SAML-based access management, organizations can keep authentication tied to their broader identity systems. This supports a more streamlined approach to user access, especially for teams that need tighter administrative oversight.
