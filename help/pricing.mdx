## FAQs

<ExpandableGroup>
  <Expandable title="When does billing start?">
    Billing usually starts when your free trial ends or when you move onto a paid plan. The exact start date should appear in your billing settings or checkout summary.
  </Expandable>

  <Expandable title="Can I choose monthly or annual billing?">
    Yes. Many plans offer both monthly and annual billing options. Annual billing is often used by teams that want simpler budgeting or discounted long-term pricing.
  </Expandable>

  <Expandable title="Can I change plans later?">
    Yes. You can usually upgrade or downgrade at any time, although the timing of the change may affect when the new rate appears on your invoice.
  </Expandable>

  <Expandable title="Where can I find my invoices and receipts?">
    Invoices and receipts are typically available from your billing or account settings. Workspace owners or billing admins usually have access to download them.
  </Expandable>

  <Expandable title="Are taxes or additional fees included in the listed price?">
    Taxes, regulatory fees, or currency adjustments may be added depending on your billing location and payment method. Your checkout page or invoice should show the final total clearly.
  </Expandable>

  <Expandable title="What happens when my free trial ends?">
    When a free trial ends, you are usually asked to choose a paid plan to continue using premium features. If no paid plan is selected, your workspace may move to a limited plan or lose access to certain features.
  </Expandable>

  <Expandable title="What happens after I cancel my subscription?">
    After cancellation, access typically continues until the end of the current billing period unless your contract says otherwise. Some features may be limited after the plan fully expires.
  </Expandable>
</ExpandableGroup>
