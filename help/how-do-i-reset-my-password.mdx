# How do I reset my password?

If you've forgotten your password or need to change it for security reasons, AetherFlow provides a secure password reset process. Follow these steps to regain access to your account.

## Password Reset Process

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit the login page">
    Go to [aetherflow.com/login](https://aetherflow.com/login)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click forgot password">
    Click the "Forgot Password?" link below the login form
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your email">
    Provide the email address associated with your AetherFlow account
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check your email">
    Look for a password reset email from AetherFlow
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click reset link">
    Click the secure reset link in the email
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create new password">
    Enter a strong, secure password following our guidelines
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm reset">
    Your password is now updated and you can log in
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Password Requirements

<Callout type="info">
  **Strong Password Guidelines:**
  - Minimum 8 characters long
  - Include uppercase and lowercase letters
  - Include at least one number
  - Include at least one special character (!@#$%^&*)
  - Avoid common words or personal information
</Callout>

Your new password must meet these security requirements to protect your account and workflows.

## Troubleshooting Reset Issues

### Email Not Received

If you don't receive the password reset email:

- **Check spam/junk folder** - Security emails sometimes get filtered
- **Wait 5-10 minutes** - Email delivery can be delayed
- **Verify email address** - Ensure you entered the correct email
- **Check email settings** - Make sure your inbox isn't full

### Reset Link Expired

Password reset links expire after 24 hours for security:

- **Request a new reset** - Start the process again
- **Use the latest email** - Only the most recent reset link works
- **Complete quickly** - Don't leave the reset process unfinished

### Account Issues

If you're having other account access problems:

- **Multiple failed attempts** may temporarily lock your account
- **Contact support** if you can't access your email
- **Verify account status** - Check if your account is active

## Security Best Practices

<Callout type="warning">
  **Password Security Tips:**

  - **Use unique passwords** for each service
  - **Enable two-factor authentication** when available
  - **Change passwords regularly** (every 3-6 months)
  - **Never share passwords** with others
  - **Use a password manager** for secure storage
</Callout>

### Account Recovery

To make password recovery easier in the future:

- **Keep recovery email current** - Update if your email changes
- **Add backup email** - Set up secondary recovery options
- **Save recovery codes** - For accounts with 2FA
- **Document security questions** - If used for account recovery

## Two-Factor Authentication

For enhanced security, consider enabling 2FA:

- **Available on Pro and Enterprise plans**
- **Works with authenticator apps** (Google Authenticator, Authy)
- **SMS backup option** for additional security
- **Hardware keys support** for maximum security

## Enterprise SSO

If your organization uses Single Sign-On:

- **Contact your IT admin** - They manage SSO access
- **Use company credentials** - No separate AetherFlow password needed
- **SSO bypass available** - For emergency access if needed

## Getting Help

If you're still having trouble:

**For password reset issues:**
- Try the reset process again
- Contact support at [support@aetherflow.com](mailto:support@aetherflow.com)
- Include your account email in the request

**For account access problems:**
- Provide as much detail as possible
- Include any error messages you see
- Mention recent account changes

**For security concerns:**
- Contact security at [security@aetherflow.com](mailto:security@aetherflow.com)
- Report suspicious activity immediately
