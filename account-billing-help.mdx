# Account & Billing Management

Manage your AetherFlow account, billing information, and subscription settings. This guide covers everything from password resets to subscription management.

## Account Security

### Password Management

**Resetting Your Password**

**Step 1: Initiate Reset**<br/>
Click "Forgot Password" on the login page and enter your email address.

**Step 2: Check Your Email**<br/>
Look for an email from AetherFlow with password reset instructions.

**Step 3: Create New Password**<br/>
Follow the link in the email to set your new password.

**Tip:** Check your spam/junk folder if you don't see the reset email within 5 minutes.

### Account Security Best Practices

**Strong Passwords**<br/>
Use complex passwords with uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and symbols.<br/>
Minimum 8 characters recommended.

**Two-Factor Authentication**<br/>
Add an extra layer of security to your account.<br/>
Available for Pro and Enterprise plans.

**Session Management**<br/>
Monitor and revoke active sessions.<br/>
View all devices logged into your account.

**Login Notifications**<br/>
Get alerts for new login attempts.<br/>
Receive emails for unrecognized logins.

## Data Management

### Exporting Your Data

AetherFlow provides comprehensive data export options for your account:

**Workflow Export**<br/>
Download workflow definitions as JSON files.<br/>
Includes trigger conditions, steps, and settings.

**Execution Logs Export**<br/>
Export workflow execution history and analytics data.<br/>
Includes timestamps, success/failure status, and performance metrics.

**Complete Account Export**<br/>
Full data export for GDPR compliance or account migration.<br/>
Includes all workflows, settings, and historical data.

### How to Export Data

1. Go to Settings → Data & Privacy
2. Click "Export Data" section
3. Select the type of export you need
4. Choose date range (if applicable)
5. Click "Generate Export"
6. Download the export file when ready

**Note:** Export processing may take a few minutes for large accounts. You'll receive an email notification when your export is ready.

## Subscription Management

### Canceling Your Subscription

**How to Cancel**

**Step 1: Access Billing Settings**<br/>
Navigate to Settings → Billing → Cancel Subscription.

**Step 2: Confirm Cancellation**<br/>
Review the cancellation details and confirm your decision.

**Step 3: Access Period**<br/>
Your account remains active until the end of your current billing period.

**Important:** You can reactivate your subscription at any time during the access period without losing your data.

### Reactivation Process

If you change your mind after canceling:

- **During access period**: Reactivate instantly in your account settings
- **After access period ends**: Sign up again with the same email address
- **Data preservation**: All workflows and data are restored upon reactivation
- **Billing**: Resumes at the next billing cycle
