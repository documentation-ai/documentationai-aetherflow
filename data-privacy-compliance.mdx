## Data Encryption

Multiple layers of encryption protect your data throughout its lifecycle.

<Columns cols={3}>
  <Card title="At Rest" icon="database">
    AES-256 encryption for all stored data using envelope encryption.
  </Card>
  <Card title="In Transit" icon="wifi">
    TLS 1.3 encryption for all data transmission with perfect forward secrecy.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Application Layer" icon="lock">
    Additional encryption for sensitive fields like API keys and tokens.
  </Card>
</Columns>

<Expandable title="Encryption Architecture">
- **Database Encryption**: Transparent Data Encryption (TDE) with AES-256
- **File Storage**: Server-side encryption with customer-managed keys (Enterprise)
- **Backup Encryption**: All backups encrypted with unique keys
- **Key Management**: Hardware Security Modules (HSMs) for key storage and rotation
</Expandable>

## Data Retention

Clear policies govern how long different types of data are retained.

| Data Type | Retention Period | Reason |
|-----------|------------------|--------|
| **Account Data** | Until account deletion | Service provision and compliance |
| **Workflow Logs** | 90 days (7 years for Enterprise) | Debugging and analytics |
| **Execution Data** | 30 days (customizable for Enterprise) | Performance monitoring |
| **Audit Logs** | 7 years | Regulatory compliance |
| **Backup Data** | 30 days after deletion | Recovery purposes |

<Expandable title="Data Deletion">
- **Account Deletion**: All associated data permanently deleted within 30 days
- **Workflow Deletion**: Data removed immediately, backups cleared after 30 days
- **GDPR Right to Erasure**: Data deleted upon verified request within 30 days
</Expandable>

## CCPA Compliance

Compliance with the California Consumer Privacy Act for California residents.

<ExpandableGroup>
  <Expandable title="CCPA Rights">
    - **Right to Know**: What personal information we collect and how it's used
    - **Right to Delete**: Request deletion of personal information
    - **Right to Opt-Out**: Opt-out of sale of personal information
    - **Right to Non-Discrimination**: No different treatment for exercising rights
  </Expandable>

  <Expandable title="CCPA Implementation">
    - No sale of personal information to third parties
    - Clear privacy notices with CCPA-specific disclosures
    - Dedicated CCPA request processing portal
    - Annual privacy reports for California residents
  </Expandable>
</ExpandableGroup>

## SOC 2 Compliance

Type II SOC 2 compliance demonstrating our security and privacy controls.

<Expandable title="SOC 2 Trust Services Criteria">
- **Security**: Protection against unauthorized access and data breaches
- **Availability**: System availability and resilience
- **Processing Integrity**: Accuracy and completeness of data processing
- **Confidentiality**: Protection of confidential information
- **Privacy**: Appropriate collection, use, and disclosure of personal information
</Expandable>

<Expandable title="SOC 2 Controls">
- Access controls and multi-factor authentication
- Regular security assessments and penetration testing
- Incident response and breach notification procedures
- Change management and configuration controls
- Continuous monitoring and logging
</Expandable>

## Incident Response

Our procedures for handling security incidents and data breaches.

  <Step title="Detection" icon="search">

  <Step title="Containment" icon="shield">

  <Step title="Recovery" icon="refresh-cw">
